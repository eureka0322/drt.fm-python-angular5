About

A podcast show for designers, developers and entrepreneurs. The show interviews the biggest names with a focus on learning. The host, William Channer is a partner at Panda Network and co-founded Panda. He co-authored Ways to Connect and has written for the Guardian. He graduated from Imperial College London and now lives in Berlin.

500 Startups

Dorm Room Tycoon has partnered with 500 Startups with the vision to create a large database of useful interviews for the next generation of creators. 500 Startups is a leading global venture capital seed fund and startup accelerator which manage 200M in assets and invest in 1300+ technology start-ups.
